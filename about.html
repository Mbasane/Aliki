About Akili Timber

Akili Timber is a sawmill based in Richmond, in the heart of KZN Midlands situated on 1.7592 hectares of land. We have the best location and have been known for saw-milling for over 13 years. We have skilled personnel in saw-milling who are community residents. Currently, we support over 10 local timber suppliers. Our Company is 100% owned and managed by PINDIWE FILTANE, who has 14 years experiences in executive level.

Our Mission

To create more employment opportunities and contribute in uplifting small businesses of the Richmond rural communities.

Our Vision

To be a highly respected sawmill in producing top quality wooden products within agreed timelines to our clients.
